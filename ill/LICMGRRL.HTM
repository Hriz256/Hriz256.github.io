This file contains information for Orcad network licensing customers.

If you are having difficulty installing your network license software, there is online documentation available to help. The Orcad Network Licensing Guide and the FlexLM End User Manual (for FLEXid users) are available for your reference. To access an online book (.PDF), you must have the Adobe Acrobat Reader installed on your system.

When you install Orcad products, you are also asked if you want to install Acrobat Reader, if you do not already have Acrobat Reader on your system. If you have an earlier version of Acrobat Reader on your system, you will need to uninstall the earlier version and reboot your system before installing Acrobat Reader 4.05.

Follow these steps to install Acrobat Reader:

  1. From the Start menu, click Run.
  2. Click Browse.
  3. Enter the following path: N:\Adobe\Setup.exe (where "N" is the letter of your CD drive).
  4. Click OK.
  5. Follow the instructions on the screen.

Follow these steps to view the Network Licensing Guide:

  1. Place the Orcad family Release 9.2 installation CD into your CD drive.
  2. From the Start menu, point to Programs.
  3. Point to the Orcad family Release 9.2 program group (or whatever you chose to name the Orcad program group during installation), then click Online Manuals. The Online Manuals and Quick Reference Cards menu opens in Acrobat Reader.
  4. Click Orcad Network Licensing Guide or FlexLM End User Manual.

Alternatively, you can:
  1. Place the Orcad family Release 9.2 installation CD into your CD drive.
  2. Click Browse Software.
  3. Double-click the DOCUMENT folder.
  4. Double-click NETLICGD.PDF to open the Network Licensing Guide or FLEXLM.PDF to open the FlexLM End User Manual.
The manuals and help files that accompany your Orcad software also contain essential information for your assistance.
